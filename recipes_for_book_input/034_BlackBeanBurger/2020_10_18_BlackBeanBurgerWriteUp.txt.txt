
 * 1 Can Black Beans, Rinsed and Drained, mashed

 * 3/4 Cup, cooked Quinoa
 * 3 oz Tomato Paste.
 * 1 tsp Paprika
 * 1 tsp onion powder
 * 1/2 tsp garlic powder
 * 1/2 tsp oregeno 
 * Salt and Pepper to taste
 * 1/3 cup breadcrumbs

 * Mix all ingredients together well
 * form into hamburger shaped patties
 * Fry for about 4 minutes on each side on medium-high heat.
