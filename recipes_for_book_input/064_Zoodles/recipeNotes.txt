4-5 zucchini
Red pepper flakes
Garlic
Olive Oil
Pasta Sauce

Zoodle the zucchini, picking out large pieces of skin.
Heat the oil in the frying pan on medium heat.
lighly cook the zoodles with red pepper flakes and garlic - 3 to 5 minutes.
Add in pasta sauce, and cook until heated.
Season to taste.
