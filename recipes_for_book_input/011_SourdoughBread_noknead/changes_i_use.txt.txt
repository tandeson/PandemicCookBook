
Do dry ingreadents first:
* Add in 10g - 25g bakers specail dry milk
* Add in 20g - 60g potato flour
* Lower Salt (10g - 14g)

Optional:
* Add in Artisan Bread Topping ( King Arthur): mix of flax, toasted sesame, black caraway, midget sunflower, poppy, and anise seeds

Directions:
* Extend rest in the fridge to 48 hrs or more
* don't use the covered dish for baking - but do use water in a cast iron frying pan to get a better crust.
* baking time is at 45m - 50m
