One of Bilyana's 

3 leeks, thinily sliced
4 oz package of mushrooms ( 1 small box )
1/4 tsp thyme
1 unbaked pastry shell - 9 in
1 cup, shredded chedder
4 eggs
1 12 oz can evoperated milk
1/2 tsp salt
pepper to tate
1/8 - 1/4 ground nutmeg
2 tsp butter or olive oil

Shape pastry shell in a pie dish.
preheat oven to 425 deg F.
in a skillet, heat oil. Then saute leeks and mushrooms until tender.
Stir in the thyme
Place mixture in pastry shell.
sprinkle with cheese
in a bowl - whisk eggs, milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg. 
Pour over the mixture in the pastry shell.
bake for 15 minutes.
Reduce heat to 375 deg f, and bake for 20 - 25 minutes. 
let stand at least 10 minutes before cutting.
